Privacy Policy for SoftLabs IIUC

Effective Date: February 2025

SoftLabs IIUC is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: Our app collects and uses real-time location data to provide **bus tracking services**. Your location is used only while sharing your bus route with other users.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Providing real-time **bus tracking** services.
  • Ensuring **accurate live tracking** for students.

3. Location Data Usage

SoftLabs IIUC requires **background location access** to ensure the bus tracking feature works even when the app is not in use.

  • Location is used only for tracking and not stored permanently.
  • Users can disable location tracking at any time in their device settings.
  • We **do not sell or share** location data with third parties.

4. Sharing of Information

We do **not sell or share** your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law.
  • With Firebase for real-time tracking and cloud storage.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no method is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your location data is stored **temporarily** and deleted automatically after a certain period. Users can request data deletion by contacting us.

7. User Rights

  • Users can disable location access from device settings.
  • Users can request data deletion at any time.
